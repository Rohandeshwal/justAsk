Plumber

A plumber is a tradesperson who specializes in installing and maintaining systems used for potable (drinking) water, sewage and drainage in plumbing systems.

Electrician

An electrician is a tradesman specializing in electrical wiring of buildings, transmission lines, stationary machines, and related equipment.

Plantation

A plantation is a large-scale estate meant for farming that specializes in cash crops. The crops that are grown include cotton, coffee, tea, cocoa, sugar cane
